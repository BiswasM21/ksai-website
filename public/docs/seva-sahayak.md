# Seva Sahayak — Autonomous Healthcare Robot

**Product by:** Kalinga Sovereign AI Pvt. Ltd.
**Website:** https://kalinga-ai.com/#seva-sahayak

## Overview

Seva Sahayak is an autonomous AI-powered robot designed for hospital environments. It operates in ICU and general ward settings, assisting with patient monitoring, pill delivery, and nurse support.

---

## Capabilities

### 1. Medicine Dispensary
Dispenses prescribed medications with dosage verification and patient ID confirmation. Reduces dispensing errors and wait times at hospital pharmacies.

### 2. Smart Reception
Handles patient check-in, appointment reminders, and queue management — speaks with visitors at the entrance and guides them to the right department.

### 3. Indoor Delivery
Delivers medicines, lab reports, and supplies across floors and wings. Autonomous navigation with obstacle avoidance and lift integration.

### 4. No-Touch Vital Signs
Measures heart rate, temperature, SpO2, and blood pressure without contact. Results displayed on-screen and sent directly to the hospital's EMR.

### 5. Multi-Language Support
Communicates fluently in English, Hindi, Odia, and regional languages — making healthcare accessible to patients from diverse linguistic backgrounds.
