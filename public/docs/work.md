## Healthcare — Patient Query AI Assistant

**Problem:** A 50-bed hospital was losing patient satisfaction due to 10-minute average wait times on phone queries.

**Solution:** Deployed a RAG-based assistant trained on hospital SOPs, FAQs, and appointment data. Integrated with WhatsApp and their website.

**Outcome:** 72% reduction in call volume. 4.6/5 patient satisfaction rating.

**Stack:** RAG, WhatsApp API, FastAPI, PostgreSQL

## Manufacturing — Inventory Forecasting Engine

**Problem:** A mid-size manufacturer was sitting on 30% excess raw material inventory due to manual, spreadsheet-based forecasting.

**Solution:** Built an ML forecasting pipeline with demand prediction, supplier lead-time modeling, and automated reorder alerts.

**Outcome:** 22% inventory reduction. 3x faster planning cycles.

**Stack:** Python, XGBoost, Airflow, Tableau

## E-commerce — AI-Powered Product Categorization

**Problem:** A 10,000+ SKU catalog required manual tagging. New products took 2 days to list correctly.

**Solution:** Fine-tuned a vision + text model for automatic product tagging, attribute extraction, and SEO description generation.

**Outcome:** Listing time reduced from 2 days to 15 minutes per product. 94% tagging accuracy.

**Stack:** PyTorch, CLIP, Next.js, Shopify API
